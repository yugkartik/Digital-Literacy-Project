Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist (For Students in India)

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone — even if they claim to be from the bank.
Remember: Entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.
Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve unexpected payment requests.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
Always verify the identity of buyers/sellers before making online transactions.
Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) — avoid third-party or unknown apps.
Enable app lock and two-factor authentication for banking and payment apps.
Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for unusual activity.
Do not share personal details like Aadhaar, PAN, or bank details on untrusted websites.
Use secure internet connections — avoid making payments on public Wi-Fi.
In case of fraud:

Report immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call Helpline: 1930 (24x7) for quick action
