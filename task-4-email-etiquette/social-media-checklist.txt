Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

--> Do’s
Think before you post — make sure your content is appropriate and respectful.

Protect your privacy — keep personal information like address, phone number, and passwords secure.

Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

Verify information before sharing — avoid spreading fake news or rumors.

Maintain a positive digital footprint — post content that reflects your values and professionalism.

--> Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal details publicly.

Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling.

Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.

Don’t overshare your location or daily routine.

Don’t accept friend/follow requests from unknown or suspicious accounts
